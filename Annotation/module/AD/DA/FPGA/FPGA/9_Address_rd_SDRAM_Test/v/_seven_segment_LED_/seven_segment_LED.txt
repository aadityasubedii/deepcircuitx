Module-level comment: The 'seven_segment_LED' module controls six 7-segment LED displays, translating a 20-bit input 'num' into individual digits for each display ('HEX5' to 'HEX0'). It operates in sync with a clock 'clk' and supports asynchronous reset 'rst_n'. Internally, a register 'lnum' stores 'num', while an always block updates display outputs using a segmented lookup 'ctable' for digit decoding, triggered on clock edges or reset.
