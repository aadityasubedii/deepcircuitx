Module-level comment: The `fiftyfivenm_adcblock_top_wrapper` module integrates a 55 nm technology ADC into digital systems, synchronizing via `clkin_from_pll_c0`, initiating conversions with `soc`, and handling low-power states (`usr_pwd`) and temperature sensing (`tsen`). It outputs the ADC's clock (`clkout_adccore`), `eoc` signal, and 12-bit converted data (`dout`). Internally, it translates channel selections (5-bit `chsel`) for the ADC and configures the core ADC instance (`fiftyfivenm_adcblock_primitive_wrapper`) with various operational parameters.
