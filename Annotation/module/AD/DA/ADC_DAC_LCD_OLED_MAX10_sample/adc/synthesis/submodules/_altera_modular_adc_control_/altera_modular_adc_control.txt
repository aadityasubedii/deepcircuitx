Module-level comment: The 'altera_modular_adc_control' module manages an ADC on Altera FPGA, handling configuration, conversion initiation, and data interfacing. It uses an FSM ('altera_modular_adc_control_fsm') for command processing and 'fiftyfivenm_adcblock_top_wrapper' for ADC operations, facilitated by input/output ports for command signals and conversion data, and internal signals for timing and control.
