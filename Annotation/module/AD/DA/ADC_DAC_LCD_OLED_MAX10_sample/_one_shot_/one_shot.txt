Module-level comment: The one_shot module generates a single-cycle pulse on 'dout' upon detecting specific edge transitions on 'din', synchronized to the 'clk'. It leverages a parameterized shift register 'buff' to track 'din' history, allowing edge detection based on the EDGE parameter (0 for rising, 1 for falling, 2 for either edge). The module dynamically configures 'buff' size with PULSE_WIDTH and utilizes combinational logic to output pulses according to edge transitions.
