This block detects the falling edge of a signal. It works by performing a bitwise AND operation on the `found_edge` and `last_test_clk` signals. When `found_edge` and `last_test_clk` are both high `(1'b1)`, this indicates a falling edge, and `falling_edge` is accordingly set high `(1'b1)`.
