Module-level comment: The 'singen_to_effects' module acts as a real-time sound effects processor using a generated sine wave. Through a synchronous design based on a clock input 'clk', it channels the generated sine wave via a 12-bit internal wire 'sin_to_eff' to various effect-processing blocks: Overdrive ('OD'), Delay ('DEL'), Reverb ('rev'), and Echo ('ech'). The processed waveforms are sent to their respective output ports: 'data_out_sin', 'data_out_OD', 'data_out_DELAY', 'data_out_REVERB', 'data_out_ECHO'.
