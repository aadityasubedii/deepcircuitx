Module-level comment: The 'controlador' module operates as a sequential controller for external units like a gain unit, DAC, and ADC, based on a locally managed 'contador' counter. On each falling edge of the input 'clock' signal, 'contador' gets incremented. Depending on 'contador', the module enables 'enablegain', 'enableadc', or 'enabledac' serially over different clock cycle ranges for operation of corresponding units. The sequence restarts post completion. Outputs 'spissb', 'sf_ce0', 'fpgainitb' are initialized but remain unaltered.
