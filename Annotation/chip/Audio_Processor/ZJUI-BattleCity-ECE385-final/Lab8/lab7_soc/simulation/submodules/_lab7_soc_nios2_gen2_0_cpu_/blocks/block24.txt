This block determines if destination control should be ignored. This is done by assigning the bitwise OR of several operations to the `D_ctrl_ignore_dst` variable. The operations considered are branch, greater/equal, less than, not equal, equal, unsigned greater/equal, unsigned less than, store byte, store halfword, store word, store byte IO, store halfword IO, store word IO, and jump and link.
