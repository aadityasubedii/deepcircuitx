Comment: 
The function "center" works to calculate a center value based on its input parameters: 'left', 'diff', and 'base'. It concatenates a zero-bit with 'left' and depending on whether the sum of this and 'diff' is smaller than twice of 'base', it either increments the sum by a static 32-bit number or reduces it by twice of 'base'. The calculation result, constrained by TAPCNTRWIDTH, is then returned as the center value.
