Module-level comment: The 'audio_echo_effect_top' module creates echo effects on audio inputs. It receives audio via an I2S interface and uses 'serial_audio_decoder', 'dual_clock_buffer' and 'audio_echo_effect' sub-modules to decode and process the audio signals. It uses signals like 'reset', 'clk', and others for controlling procedures and indicating status for data transfers across different clock domains. The 'spdif_audio_encoder' finally converts the processed audio into SPDIF output. Control inputs guide the data flow and enable reset operations.
