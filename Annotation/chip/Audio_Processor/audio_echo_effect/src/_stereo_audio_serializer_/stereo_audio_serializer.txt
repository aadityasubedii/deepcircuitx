Module-level comment: Module `stereo_audio_serializer` serializes stereo audio input. Inputs are valid with high `i_valid` and `i_ready`. `left` and `right` internal registers store left and right audio inputs. On triggering `o_valid` and `o_ready`, it alternates output between `left` and `right` based on `o_is_left`. `i_ready` is the inverse of `o_is_left`, readying the module for new input when right audio is outputted. After a reset, the module becomes ready for input and defaults to left audio output.
