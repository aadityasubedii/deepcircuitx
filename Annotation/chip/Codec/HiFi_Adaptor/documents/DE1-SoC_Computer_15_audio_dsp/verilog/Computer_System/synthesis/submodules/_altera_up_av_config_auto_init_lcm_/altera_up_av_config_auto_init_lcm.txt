Module-level comment: The `altera_up_av_config_auto_init_lcm` module initializes LCD configurations by mapping preset parameters to ROM addresses. It accepts a 5-bit `rom_address`, returning a 16-bit `rom_data` configured from internal 14-bit `data`, combining a 6-bit identifier and 8-bit parameter value. Implementation includes a `case` statement directed by `rom_address`, outputting configured `rom_data` for specific LCD settings.
