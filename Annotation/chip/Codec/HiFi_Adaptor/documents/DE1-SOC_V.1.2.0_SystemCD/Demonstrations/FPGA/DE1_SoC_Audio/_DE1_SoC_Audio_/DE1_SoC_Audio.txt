Module-level comment: The DE1_SoC_Audio module controls audio and peripheral functionalities on DE1-SoC board, interfacing with ADCs, DACs, SDRAM, HEX displays, and LEDs. It utilizes input clocks and various I/O ports for data transfer and device management. Internal signals like HEXPx manage display processing, while the audio_nios submodule orchestrates the core audio handling and I/O operations, ensuring synchronized audio processing with efficient memory and peripheral interaction.
