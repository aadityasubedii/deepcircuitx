Module-level comment: The SEG7_LUT_6 module converts a 32-bit input (`iDIG`) into segment signals for six 7-segment displays (oSEG0 to oSEG5). It uses six instances of the `SEG7_LUT` submodule to decode each 4-bit slice of `iDIG` into the respective 7-bit display output. Each submodule directly maps a segment of `iDIG` to its corresponding display output, facilitating the visualization of hexadecimal characters on the displays.
