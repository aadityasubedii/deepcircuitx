Module name: codec2_encoder_2400. Module specification: This module implements a CODEC2 speech encoder for the 2400 bps mode, processing speech samples in frames and encoding them into compressed bits. It takes inputs start_codec2, clk, and rst to control the encoding process, and outputs encoded_bits_0 and encoded_bits_1 (48-bit registers for two frames of encoded data), quant0 to quant9 (5-bit registers for quantized LSP coefficients), c_encode_woe, c_nlp_pitch1, c_nlp_pitch2 (32-bit registers for encoding parameters), and done_codec2 (signal indicating completion).
