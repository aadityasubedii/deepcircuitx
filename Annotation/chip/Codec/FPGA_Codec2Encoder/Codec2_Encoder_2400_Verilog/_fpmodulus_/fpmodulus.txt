Module-level comment: The 'fpmodulus' module computes the modulus of two inputs using a finite state machine (FSM). It initiates with the 'startfmod' signal, progressing through states like SET_MOD, CHECK_IF, and CALC_MOD using 'qadd' and 'fpgreaterthan' for arithmetic and comparisons. Results are delivered via 'mod' and 'donefmod' indicating completion.
