Module name: codec2_encoder_2400_one_frame. Module specification: This module implements a codec2 voice compression encoder for 2400 bps encoding of a single frame of audio data. It takes inputs start_oneframe, clk, and rst to initiate encoding, synchronize operations, and reset the module respectively. The module outputs encoded_bits (48-bit final encoded bitstream), done_oneframe (completion indicator), and various intermediate results including out_mem_x, out_mem_y, out_prevf0, out_xq0, out_xq1, c_encode_model_wo, c_pitch1, c_pitch2, c_cmax1, and c_cmax2.
