Module-level comment: The 'mclkgen' module generates two clocks, 'mclk' and 'bclk', based on the primary 'clk' and 'reset'. Using internal registers 'estat' and 'bcont', the module increments 'estat' on each clock edge, resetting on 'reset'. 'bclk' is linked to 'bcont' which only increments when 'estat' is 0. Outputs 'mclk' and 'bclk' derive from the highest bits of 'estat' and 'bcont', respectively, dictating their frequencies and transition behaviors based on module state logic.
