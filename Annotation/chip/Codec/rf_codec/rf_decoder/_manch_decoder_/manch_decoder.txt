Module-level comment: The manch_decoder module decodes Manchester encoded signals using a clk2x input for double rate clock needs. It features a shift register fifo for data sampling, synchronization flag syn_flg, output buffers dout_buf and error_buf, and a toggling clock clk1x. Decoding is controlled by enable input, switching outputs dout and error between data/error indications and high impedance states depending on enable status.
