Module-level comment: The 'rf_encoder' module encodes a digital input 'din' into three encoding schemes: Manchester, Miller, and FM0, controlled by 'enable', synchronized with a 'clk2x', and initialized by 'rst_n'. It uses submodules 'manchester_encoder', 'miller_encoder', and 'fmiller_encoder' for each respective encoding, outputting 'manch_out', 'miller_out', and 'fmiller_out'. Each encoder shares input signals and independently produces its encoding.
