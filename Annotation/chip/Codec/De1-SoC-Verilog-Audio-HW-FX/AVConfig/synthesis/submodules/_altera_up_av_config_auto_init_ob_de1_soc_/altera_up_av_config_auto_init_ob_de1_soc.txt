Module-level comment: The `altera_up_av_config_auto_init_ob_de1_soc` module initializes audio and video settings for DE1-SoC FPGA boards. It inputs a ROM address and outputs combined audio/video configurations. Internal signals `audio_rom_data` and `video_rom_data` fetch respective settings from audio and video ROM modules, merging them with a bitwise OR for the output.
