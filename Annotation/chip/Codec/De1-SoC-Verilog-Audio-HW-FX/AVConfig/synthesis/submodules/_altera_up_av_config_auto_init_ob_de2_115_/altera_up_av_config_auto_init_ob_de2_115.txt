Module-level comment: The `altera_up_av_config_auto_init_ob_de2_115` module integrates audio and video configuration settings for FPGA multimedia setups by combining configuration data from two ROM modules. It accepts a 6-bit `rom_address` to fetch specific settings, and outputs a 27-bit `rom_data` through logical OR of `audio_rom_data` and `video_rom_data` from `Auto_Init_Audio_ROM` and `Auto_Init_Video_ROM`. This facilitates synchronized audio-video initialization.
