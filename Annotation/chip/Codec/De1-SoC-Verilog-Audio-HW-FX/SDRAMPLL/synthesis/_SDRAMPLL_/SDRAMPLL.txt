Module-level comment: The SDRAMPLL module interfaces with a PLL for generating SDRAM and system clocks from a reference clock. It takes `ref_clk_clk` and `ref_reset_reset` as inputs and outputs `reset_source_reset`, `sdram_clk_clk`, and `sys_clk_clk`. This module encapsulates the `SDRAMPLL_sys_sdram_pll_0` instantiation, forwarding inputs directly to outputs, thus serving as a seamless interface for SDRAM-related clock management.
