Module-level comment: The SDRAMPLL_sys_sdram_pll_0 generates system and SDRAM-specific clocks using a PLL. It inputs a reference clock (`ref_clk_clk`) and a reset signal (`ref_reset_reset`). Outputs include two clocks (`sys_clk_clk`, `sdram_clk_clk`) and a managed reset (`reset_source_reset`) governed by PLL lock status via `sys_pll_locked_export`, ensuring stability before operation.
