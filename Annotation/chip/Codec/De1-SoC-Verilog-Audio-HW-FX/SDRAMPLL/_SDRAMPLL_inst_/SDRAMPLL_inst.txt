Module-level comment: The SDRAMPLL module synchronizes system and SDRAM operations through a PLL based on a reference clock input (`ref_clk_clk`). It accepts a reset (`ref_reset_reset`) and outputs system (`sys_clk_clk`), SDRAM (`sdram_clk_clk`), and reset signals (`reset_source_reset`). This setup ensures coordinated reset and clock synchronization critical for robust SDRAM functioning.
