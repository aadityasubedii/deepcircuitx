Module-level comment: The 'altera_up_av_config_auto_init_ob_de1_soc' module initializes audio and video settings on the DE1-SoC board. It takes a 6-bit 'rom_address' to read from two ROMs—'Auto_Init_Audio_ROM' and 'Auto_Init_Video_ROM'—using this address. The outputs from these ROMs, 'audio_rom_data' and 'video_rom_data', are combined via an OR operation to produce a 27-bit 'rom_data' output that configures the board’s audio and video settings.
