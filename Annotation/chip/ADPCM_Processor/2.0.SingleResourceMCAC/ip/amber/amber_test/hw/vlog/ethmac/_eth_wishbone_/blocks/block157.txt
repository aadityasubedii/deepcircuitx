This block of Verilog code primarily manages data transmission and reception. It includes registers ("reg") for write-back acknowledgement and for latching on received statuses. It also uses these registers to enable or disable (WbEn, WbEn_q) certain write-back functionalities and furthermore, monitoring the states of receive (RxEn, RxEn_q) and transmit (TxEn, TxEn_q) operations. They are also used to store the immediate past state (r_TxEn_q and r_RxEn_q) of the receive and transmit operations.
