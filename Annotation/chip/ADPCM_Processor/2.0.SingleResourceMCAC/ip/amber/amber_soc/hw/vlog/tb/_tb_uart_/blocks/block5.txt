This block handles the reset and update of the `tx_push_r` signal in a UART transmitter module. At the rising edge of `clk_uart` or the falling edge of `clk_uart_rst_n`, it checks if `clk_uart_rst_n` is not active (low); if so, it resets `tx_push_r` to 0. Otherwise, it simply passes the `tx_push` signal to `tx_push_r`.
